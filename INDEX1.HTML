Refund & Cancellation Policy

CANCELLATION POLICY

You may cancel your booking by notifying us in writing (email or WhatsApp). Refund eligibility is based on the notice period before the scheduled tour date:

Time of Cancellation Refund Policy
More than 7 days 90% refund of total price or full credit voucher
3-7 days 50% refund of total price
Less than 3 days No refund
No show on tour date No refund; no reschedule available

RESCHEDULING POLICY

  • • Rescheduling is allowed once, requested at least 3 days before the original date.
  • • Rescheduled bookings are non-refundable.

REFUND MODE AND TIMELINE

  • • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 working days.
  • • Refunds issued via the original payment method (UPI, bank, etc.).
  • • Bank/UPI details must be correctly provided by the guest.

EXCEPTIONS

No refunds in case of:

  • • Force majeure (weather, strikes, roadblocks).
  • • Late arrival or mid-tour exit by the guest.

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

Email us at:

kremchempenortheastmeghalaya@gmail.com

Please include: Full Name, Invoice Number, Tour Date, Reason for Cancellation, and UPI/Bank details (if applicable).
